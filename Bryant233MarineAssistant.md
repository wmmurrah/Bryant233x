## 5. OPERATIONAL SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)

### Launch & Retrieval
* **Blower Protocol:** Run blower for minimum 4 minutes before key-turn. Perform "Sniff Test" of bilge.
* **Battery Switch:** Ensure switch is ON before launch.
* **Trailer Depth:** Do not submerge trailer past the fender tops (adjust for ramp steepness).

### Anchoring Strategy (The "No Beaching" Rule)
* **Depth Requirement:** Minimum 4 feet of water under the drive.
* **Method:** Two-Anchor Setup.
    1.  Drop Bow Anchor first upwind/current.
    2.  Back down to set.
    3.  Deploy Stern Anchor to prevent swing.
* **Shore Access:** Ferry passengers and gear using a towable tube or raft.
* **Swimming Safety:** ABSOLUTELY NO swimming near the stern while the engine is running (CO poisoning risk).

### Fueling
* **Procedure:** Engine OFF, Blower ON, all passengers OFF the boat.
* **Additives:** Add STA-BIL 360 *before* pumping fuel to ensure mixing.
